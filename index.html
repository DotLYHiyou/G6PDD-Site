Welcome!

This website is dedicated and designed to raise awareness about Glucose-6-Phosphate Dehydrogenase Deficiency (G6PDD). My end goal is to provide a comprehensive resource for individuals seeking information about G6PDD, its symptoms, diagnosis, management, and support resources.

G6PDD is a genetic disorder that affects the red blood cells and can lead to episodes of hemolytic anemia. It is one of the most common enzyme deficiencies worldwide, affecting millions of people. The deficiency is particularly prevalent in certain regions, including Africa, the Mediterranean, and Southeast Asia. Despite its prevalence, many people remain unaware of the condition and its implications.

The primary purpose of this site is to educate and inform the public about G6PDD. We aim to achieve this by providing Comprehensive and accurate information about G6PDD, including its causes, symptoms, diagnosis, and treatment options, which are backed up by source throughout the health industry.

We believe that by providing a reliable and informative resource, we can increase awareness about G6PDD, support those affected by the condition, and contribute to the ongoing efforts to improve the diagnosis and management of G6PDD. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of individuals and families impacted by this condition.
