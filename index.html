Welcome!

This website is dedicated and designed to raise awareness about Glucose-6-Phosphate Dehydrogenase Deficiency (G6PDD). My end goal is to provide a comprehensive resource for individuals seeking information about this condition.

G6PDD is a genetic disorder that affects the red blood cells and can lead to episodes of hemolytic anemia. It is one of the most common enzyme deficiencies worldwide, affecting millions of people.

The primary purpose of this site is to educate and inform the public about G6PDD. We aim to achieve this by providing comprehensive and accurate information about G6PDD, including its causes, symptoms, and management strategies.

We believe that by providing a reliable and informative resource, we can increase awareness about G6PDD, support those affected by the condition, and contribute to the ongoing efforts to improve their quality of life.
